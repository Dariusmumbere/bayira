Holding the local government/ duty bearers Accountable for Menstrual Hygiene Management improvement in Rural government schools.

February 9, 2024

BARWODA managed to conduct a dialogue meeting with the MHM duty bearers in the education department of Bukonzo East constituency.
Issues of establishing Menstrual Hygiene Management facilities in the rural government schools were discussed and emphasized i.e construction of wash/ change rooms for girls during their menstrual periods, water facilities, dumping buckets and incinerators, construction of segregated latrines- girls alone stances, boys stances, teachers-female and male alone stances to maintain gender privacy.
